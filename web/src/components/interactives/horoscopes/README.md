# Horoscopes Capstone (Ch. 17)

**Pedagogical goal.** Close the course by turning the tools back on the
learner. Chapter 17 has no models to fit; its lesson is that a golem has no
wisdom of its own, so the capstone is a mirror: how well-calibrated were your
guesses, which golems did you forge, and what can none of them do?

**What it shows.** Three sections, all read from the persisted workshop store:

- *Calibration report* — mean overlap across every posterior you sketched
  before a reveal, broken down by chapter, with the honest gloss that low
  scores usually mean intervals that were too narrow.
- *Bestiary review* — a compact grid of every golem, forged or unforged, with
  counts of golems forged and benches finished.
- *What your golems can't do* — four original reflective prompts with no
  answer key, each pointing at a structural limit of the models the course
  taught.

**No engine.** This is a reflective view over `calibrations`, `golems`, and
`chapters` from the store; there is no sampler or numeric claim to unit-test.
It degrades gracefully when no calibration data exists yet, pointing the
learner back to the chapter-2 globe toss.
